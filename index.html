Training Karpathy's char-rnn to talk like Snooki - What happens when AI watches TV (hint, it's not very intelligent)

Multi-layer Recurrent Neural Networks (LSTM, GRU, RNN) for character-level language models in Torch that's been trained on the script from Jersey Shore, Hannah Montana, the Lord of the Rings trilogy and David Copperfield by Charles Dickens
